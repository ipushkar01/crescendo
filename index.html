Meet the Team

Presenting the Crescendo Team for 2022-2023

Fattu Ishan Singh - President

Fattu Ishan Singh is an articulate drummer who also renders his skills for the school band- Diffuse. He is one with an amicable personality who creates a welcoming atmosphere for the junior. His management skills are praiseworthy and an asset to the society.

Simran Tavish Joshi - Vice President

Simran Tavish Joshi is blessed with the art of singing and is the soloist of AISMV & the lead vocalist of the girl band- Odd One Out. Her speaking and leadership skills make her a worthy recipient of this post. She aspires to take the team to new heights under her guidance.

Annika Arora - Secretary

Annika Arora has an incredible eye for creativity. She is the leader of Crescendo's IT Team and is the mind behind it's beautiful presentations and artworks. She also renders her voice for the school choir, which makes her the perfect candidate for this position.

Mannat Bhalla Papdi - Treasurer

Mannat Bhalla Papdi is one of the Duet Singers of AISMV who leads the harmonies sections. The team looks up to her impeccable organizational skills and outstanding musicianship. She has won many laurels for AISMV and continues to be an integral part of the society.
